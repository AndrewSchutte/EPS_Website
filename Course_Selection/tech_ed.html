Woodwork 9/10

910Elective

Course Code: MADW-09--S

Course Code: MWWK-10--S

This course is designed to build on the knowledge gained at the junior level. The student will be exposed to a wider variety of skills, tools, operational safety and tool maintenance. The knowledge gained will be applied in the design and manufacture of projects at a higher skill and maturity level. Accuracy and emphasis on detail is important in this course and will form a valuable part of the student's development.

Furniture & Cabinetry 12

12Elective

(Recommended Prerequisite: Woodwork 11 or Woodwork 12)

Course Code: MTFAC12--S

This specialty course will specifically focus on the design and creation of advanced woodcraft projects. Students will be expected to challenge themselves in creating several artistic projects in the following possible areas: wood turning, intarsia, carving, and scroll work. The student's artistic creation could be a stand-alone project or an enhancement of a value-added piece of furniture or cabinet.
